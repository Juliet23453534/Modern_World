Meaning Of "Safety Tips"

What are "Safety Tips"? They are tips to prevent you from dangerous occasions and encounters. You must obey these tips - not just in real life, on computers and online too. On this webpage, we will guide you through numerous safety tips.

Safety Tips

Don't share Personal Information

  • Avoid sharing personal information like passwords, banking details, social media account access, or addresses online.
  • Recognize that identity theft isn't the only risk of poor cyber security.

VPN Use for Wi-Fi Connection

  • VPN secures device connection to internet server and prevents monitoring or data access.
  • Legal and effective for home and public Wi-Fi use.VPN may slow internet connection due to data routing.

Double-Checking Links Before Clicking

  • Trust and know the source of any links before clicking on them. Avoid downloading compromised apps and software.
  • Mouse over a link to preview its full link in your browser's status bar.
  • Verify the correct site link by going to the company name.

Secure Public Wi-Fi Networks

  • Avoid unsecured public Wi-Fi networks unless absolutely necessary.
  • Avoid logging into accounts online or on apps, or entering personal or financial information.
  • Hackers often set up fake Wi-Fi hotspots, allowing them to monitor online activities.

Logging into Secure Sites

  • Ensure web browser address starts with https://, not http://.
  • Look for padlock symbol next to site address.
  • "S" stands for "secure," indicating data encryption.

Turning Off Bluetooth: Protect Your Device

  • Researchers discovered Bluetooth communications can be compromised
  • They exploited a vulnerability in a Bluetooth connection, altering nearby communications.
  • While not harmful, turning off Bluetooth if not paired to another device can save battery.

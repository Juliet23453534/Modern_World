Meaning Of "APP"

What is "APP"? Well, it is an apprevation for application. This may be a type of software that can be installed/downloaded and process on a computer, tablet, smartphone or other electronic devices. Almost every application has its own functions which are narrow and very specific. On this web page, we will talk about 8 famous applications and other numerous things too.

8 Famous APPS

Whatsapp

WhatsApp: Global Messaging App

  • Supports international calling.
  • Enables text, voice, and video messages.
  • Facilitates sharing of images, documents, and locations.
  • Has 2 billion global users.

Pricing: Free, Availability: iOS and Android

Facebook

Facebook: Global Social Networking App

  • Offers easy online connection and sharing.
  • Open to anyone over 13 with valid email.
  • As of 2020, 2.6 billion monthly active users worldwide.

Pricing: Free, Availability: Android, iOS, BlackBerry, and more

Instagram

Instagram: A Visual Mobile App

  • Connects users with brands, celebrities, thought leaders, friends, family, etc.
  • Over 1 billion users as of Q3 2020, 500 million daily active users of Instagram Stories.

Pricing: Free, Availability: iOS, Android, and more

Snapchat

Snapchat: A Global Popularity

  • Easy way to share everyday moments.
  • Unlike other apps, messages disappear.
  • Offers unique photo and video editing tools.
  • Founded in 2011 with 238 million users as of Q2 2020.

Pricing: Free, Availability: Android, iOS

Uber

Uber: Online Taxi/Cab Hiring App

  • Access to numerous types of rides in around 10,000 cities.
  • Shows the directions for the driver automatically as well as estimated distance and fare.(Pay with your preferred method)
  • Has over 5 billion rides and around 75 million active Uber riders are available.

Pricing: Free(Cost for Cabs is excluded), Availability: iOS and Android

YouTube

YouTube : Online Video Sharing App

  • Sprovides users with a platform to share and watch videos online.
  • All people watch around 6 billion hours of video every month
  • 100 hours of video, on average, are uploaded to YouTube every minute making it one of the most popular mobile applications.

Pricing: Free, Availability: iOS, Android & more

Google Maps

Google Maps: Mapping Service App

  • Offers satellite imagery, aerial photography, street maps, and 360Â° interactive panoramic views of streets.
  • Real-time traffic conditions and route planning services can also be availed with the help of this app.
  • On Play Store, the Google Maps app has over 5 billion downloads.

Pricing: Free, Availability: iOS, Android & more

Twitter(X)

Twitter(X): Global Sending & Receiving Application

  • Allowing users to send and receive short posts called tweets and enables to follow people and organizations that you fancy.
  • Twitter was founded in 2006 and the total mobile app downloads for Twitter are 16 million in 2021.

Pricing: Free, Availability: iOS, Android & more





Recommendations

Canva

Canva is a free online graphic design tools.
It is mainly used for developing social media posts, posters, videos, logos, presentations and many more.
Pricing: Free (Can upgrade to premium with money)
Platforms: Windows, Mac, Android, iOS & More

Spotify

Spotify is a digital music, podcast, and video streaming service that provides you with millions of songs and various other content from creators around the world.
We can listen to many songs that match with your taste.
Pricing: Free (Can upgrade to premium with money)
Platforms: Windows, Mac, Android, iOS & More

Amazon

Amazon is an internet-based business retailer that is largely focused on assisting with e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming and artificial intelligence services.
Pricing: Pay for what you have bought
Platforms: Windows, Mac, Android, iOS & More

CapCut

CapCut is an all-in-one creative platform powered by artificial intelligence that allows video editing and image.
Pricing: Free (Can upgrade to premium with money)
Platforms: Windows, Mac, Android, iOS & More

Warp

Warp is a VPN that assists you to connect to the internet utilizing Cloudflare's 1.1. 1.1 DNS while simultaneously enhacing and securing (i.e. encrypting) your WIFI connection.
Pricing: Free (Can upgrade to premium with money)
Platforms: Windows, Mac, Android, iOS & More

SonicWall

SonicWall is a cybersecurity company that sells various Internet appliances directed at content control and network cybersecurity.
Pricing: Free
Platforms: Windows, Mac & More
